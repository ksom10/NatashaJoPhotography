What We Provide

OT Screening

Our screening process is a brief observation designed to give a “snapshot” of your child’s current abilities. During a 15-minute review, we gather essential information—either through a quick phone conversation or a short in-person observation—to help determine whether a full evaluation is necessary.

What to Expect:

  • Brief overview of concerns related to motor skills, sensory needs, or development
  • Discussion of immediate needs and potential next steps
  • Helps decide if a more comprehensive evaluation is appropriate

By performing this quick “snapshot,” we can easily identify any red flags or areas of concern, empowering parents and caregivers to make informed decisions about the best path forward for their child.

Request a Screening

Comprehensive Occupational Therapy Evaluations

Our comprehensive evaluation offers an in-depth look at your child’s skills and abilities. We review medical and developmental history, administer standardized or informal assessments, and compare your child’s performance to typical age expectations.

What to Expect:

  • 60-minute session (approx.) to examine areas such as strength, balance, or sensory processing
  • In-depth report summarizing findings and individualized recommendations
  • Opportunities to ask questions and collaborate on goals

Armed with this detailed information, families can make better decisions about therapy services or resources. Our ultimate goal is to provide clarity and guide you toward the most effective plan of care.

Schedule an Evaluation

Occupational Therapy Treatment Sessions

Treatment sessions are tailored to the unique needs of each child and are available in both 45‑minute and 1‑hour sessions. We offer flexible scheduling and provide therapy virtually, in‑home, at school, in the community, or at our clinic.

What to Expect:

  • Goal-based interventions aligned with evaluation results
  • Collaborative approach with teachers, caregivers, and other professionals
  • Engaging activities to improve strength, coordination, and sensory processing

Each session is designed to keep children motivated and involved. We continuously adjust therapy goals as progress is made, ensuring the best possible outcomes for your child’s development and wellbeing.

One-on-One

We also provide one-on-one sessions that focus on a child’s individualized goals. This personalized attention offers targeted strategies and faster progress, especially when compared to general group settings.

What to Expect:

  • Close guidance and hands-on techniques adapted to each child’s skill level
  • Opportunities for frequent feedback and real-time adjustments

Because every child’s situation is unique, individualized therapy can be a powerful way to address particular challenges or accelerate progress in specific areas of development.

Request One-on-One Services

Natural Environment

Our private therapy sessions can be held in your child’s familiar surroundings—whether that’s at home, daycare, or a private school. By working in the environment they’re most comfortable in, children are more likely to retain and generalize the skills they learn.

What to Expect:

  • Therapy activities blended seamlessly into daily routines
  • Increased participation and cooperation in a familiar setting
  • Greater opportunity for caregiver coaching and direct feedback

This approach often leads to enhanced consistency and better long-term results since children practice new skills right where they spend the most time.

Caregiver Coaching

We believe that parents and guardians are an essential part of any child’s growth. Our caregiver coaching model provides the tools, strategies, and ongoing support necessary for you to reinforce therapeutic gains outside of formal sessions.

What to Expect:

  • Tailored guidance on how to practice new skills at home
  • Regular check-ins and feedback to monitor progress
  • Collaborative approach for faster, more sustainable improvements

By actively involving caregivers in the therapy process, children benefit from consistent reinforcement of goals, a supportive environment, and a stronger sense of trust in their progress.
