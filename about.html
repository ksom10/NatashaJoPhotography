About Us

Kynsie Someson, OTR/L

Kynsie is a dedicated and compassionate Occupational Therapist with a passion for helping children of all ages. She earned her Master's in Occupational Therapy from Barry University and her Bachelor's of Science in Kinesiology from Indiana University, Purdue University, Indianapolis.

With a diverse range of experience across multiple settings, Kynsie has worked in homes, clinics, schools, PPECs (Prescribed Pediatric Extended Care Centers), and community environments. Her approach is child-centered and play-based, ensuring that therapy is not only effective but also engaging and enjoyable for her young clients.

Kynsie is committed to providing personalized care that empowers children to reach their full potential, focusing on their unique needs and developmental goals. Her broad experience and genuine passion for making a positive impact on children's lives make her a dedicated advocate for her clients' growth and success.

Chris Catanzaro, CEO

Chris Catanzaro is a dedicated healthcare professional with over six years of experience in healthcare administration and a strong passion for making a difference behind the scenes. A graduate of Florida Atlantic University (FAU), Chris’s career has been shaped by a deep belief that healthcare should always be centered around the patient — a philosophy that guides everything he does.

Throughout his time in the healthcare space, Chris developed a keen understanding of what families truly need: clear communication, genuine support, and systems that work for them, not against them. This commitment led him to his role as CEO of Kynetic Integrations, where he oversees the business operations and ensures that families experience the best possible care from the very first interaction.

While Chris’s day-to-day focus is on building strong operations and a supportive environment for both patients and staff, his larger vision is simple: to help as many families as possible and continue growing Kynetic Integrations into a place where every child has the opportunity to thrive.
