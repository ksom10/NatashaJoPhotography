Sensory Regulation

We focus on helping children interpret and organize sensory input, so they can feel more comfortable and engaged in daily routines without being overwhelmed by sights, sounds, or textures.

Fine Motor & Handwriting

Strengthening hand, wrist, and finger control is crucial for activities like handwriting, drawing, buttoning clothes, and more, fostering greater independence and success in the classroom and at home.

Activities of Daily Living

From dressing and bathing to feeding and grooming, we help children become more self-sufficient and confident in everyday routines.

Motor Planning & Coordination

By enhancing balance, body awareness, and movement sequencing, children learn to carry out multi-step tasks more easily—such as climbing stairs, riding a bike, or participating in sports.

Visual-Motor Integration

We address how the eyes and hands work together—improving skills needed for reading, copying, and other tasks that require coordinated visual and motor activity.
